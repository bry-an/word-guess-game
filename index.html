This is Jeopardy! - Word-Guess Game!

Instructions:

  1. Click Play Game! to view your clue. The category and value are displayed beneath the clue.
  2. If you think you know the answer, click Double! to risk double the value.
  3. Type letters to make a guess. You may submit at most five incorrect guesses for each clue, depending on the length of correct answer.
  4. If you are able to fill in all blanks, you gain the clue value (or double if clicked). If unable, you lose the same.
  5. Good luck!
